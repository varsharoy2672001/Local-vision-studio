# **App Name**: LocalVision

## Core Features:

- Video Upload: Upload video files from the local machine.
- Video Player: Display a video player interface with controls for play, pause, and volume.
- Format Support: Support common video formats such as MP4, AVI, and MOV.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft blue (#89B4FA) for a calming and focused viewing experience.
- Background color: Dark gray (#2E3440) to make the video player stand out.
- Accent color: Light orange (#D08770) to highlight interactive elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for clean and accessible readability.
- Use simple, minimalist icons for the player controls (play, pause, volume, etc.).
- Arrange the player controls in a consistent and intuitive manner for easy navigation.
- Provide subtle transitions when transitioning between video states (e.g., play/pause).
